About Us

Goffle stands for the "waffle to go", or to "grab a waffle before you go", aiming to be a tasty fast fix in your hectic academic schedule.

Our startup's story originated within our campus

We are a group of students from the University of Nottingham Malaysia, who are passionate about waffles and ice cream. We are also passionate about making the world a better place, and we believe that the best way to do so is to start with ourselves.

We are committed to using only the best ingredients, and we are committed to making sure that our products are as healthy as possible. We are also committed to making sure that our products are as affordable as possible, so that everyone can enjoy them.

Goffle is a vibrant and innovative waffle company dedicated to serving delicious and delightful waffle creations. Our three primary aims are:

  • Quality
  • Creativity
  • Customer Satisfaction

Our mission is to bring joy and indulgence to our customers through our unique and mouthwatering waffle offerings.
Shortly after opening our business, we recognized the growing demand for convenience and accessibility. That's why we swiftly introduced our delivery service to cater to our customers.
